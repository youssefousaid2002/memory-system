📱 Add to iPhone Calendar + Lockscreen Reminders

Each export only includes new lessons you haven't exported before. The file is named after what's inside. You get two notifications the day before each review: at 9am and again at 8pm.

Step 1 — Import the file
1️⃣ Click "Download Calendar File" below
2️⃣ AirDrop it to your iPhone, or email it to yourself
3️⃣ Open the file → tap "Add All" ✅

Step 2 — Make alerts stay on your lockscreen
4️⃣ Open Settings → Notifications → Calendar
5️⃣ Set "Alert Style" → Alerts (not Banners)
6️⃣ Enable "Show on Lock Screen" ✅

⚠️ "Banners" disappear after a few seconds. "Alerts" stay on your lockscreen until you tap to dismiss — that's what makes them visible all day.

Adding new lessons later?
Just add your lessons, then download again — the new file will contain only the new lessons, with no duplicates from before.

❓ How Spaced Repetition Works

Spaced Repetition is a scientifically proven technique. Instead of studying the same thing every day, you review it at the perfect moment — right before you forget it. This forces your brain to work hard each time, making the memory stronger and longer-lasting.

📋 How to Use This System

  1. Click "Add Lesson" — write your lesson title, subject, and what to memorize
  2. Click "Study Now" — read each lesson card carefully
  3. Click the card to reveal the answer
  4. If you remembered → click "I Remembered ✅" — lesson moves to next level
  5. If you forgot → click "I Forgot ❌" — lesson goes back to level 1
  6. Check "Schedule" to see exactly when each lesson is due
  7. Come back every day — your memory gets stronger each time!
